Thanneeru Amber the Tiger

Thanneeru Amber the Tiger is a majestic and adventurous character, known for its vibrant amber-colored fur and fierce personality. This courageous tiger roams through enchanted forests and embarks on thrilling expeditions in search of hidden treasures.

Games Played by Thanneeru Amber

  • Quest: Amber explores unknown territories.
  • Chase: Amber chases to catch its prey.
  • Hide-and-Seek: Amber hides in the forest, challenging friends to find it.
