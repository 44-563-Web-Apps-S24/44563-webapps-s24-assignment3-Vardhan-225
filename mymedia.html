Favourite book is Sapiens: "A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari.

This book provides a fascinating overview of human evolution, from ancient ancestors to the present day. Harari's concise and thought-provoking narrative explores key revolutions that shaped our species, offering fresh insights into the cultural and social forces that define us today.
